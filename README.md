## Feature Capabilities

The **Instagram User Collection** feature of MadHub provides two modes: **Profile Mode** and **Blogger Mode**. In **Profile Mode**, you can perform direct filtering, following, and private messaging on specified users, facilitating personalized outreach. **Blogger Mode** allows you to filter potential clients from target bloggers’ follower or following lists, broadening your reach to users who are already engaged with similar content.

This powerful feature supports various advanced filtering options, including AI avatar recognition and multi-dimensional social data filtering, ensuring you accurately target potential clients and build a robust customer database. By utilizing these capabilities, you can enhance your marketing efforts and improve the efficiency of your social media campaigns.
